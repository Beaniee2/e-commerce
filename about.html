Frequently asked questions

How do I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order by emailing your request with your order number, we can only accept cancellations up to 24hrs post order.

How do I track my order?

You will recieve all relevant tracking information in your confirmation email.

How do I obtain a copy of my statement and invoices?

Please email us and we can send these over. This can take up to 48hrs

How do I return an item?

All returns must be requested within 30days of ordering the item. This can be done via email with us
